## üìÑ Resume Creation & Management

### How to Create Your Resume:

1. **Log In** to your Nexora account
2. **Click** on "Resume" in the navigation menu
3. **Select** "Create/Edit Resume"
4. **Fill in** your information:
   - Personal details (Name, Email, Phone, Location)
   - Professional headline
   - Professional summary
   - Key skills
   - Work experience
   - Education
5. **Click** "Save Resume"
6. **View** or **Download** your resume

### Download Options:
- **Europass CV (PDF)** - International standard format
- **Standard Resume (PDF)** - Custom formatted resume

### Tips:
- Use bullet points for experience and skills
- Keep it concise and focused
- Update regularly with new skills and achievements
- Include relevant keywords for better matching

## üåç Global Job Search

### How to Search for Jobs:

1. **Go to** "Job Search" in the navigation menu
2. **Enter** a job title or keywords (e.g., "Python Developer", "Data Scientist")
3. **Enter** a location or country (e.g., "USA", "Germany", "Singapore")
4. **Click** "Search Jobs"
5. **Browse** the results
6. **View** job details or apply directly

### Apply for Jobs:

1. **Find** a job you're interested in
2. **Click** "Apply Now"
3. **Log In** (if not already logged in)
4. **Submit** your application
5. **Check** "My Applications" to track status

### Search Tips:
- Be specific with job titles for better results
- Include country names or major cities
- Try different keywords if no results found
- Use skill names as keywords

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

### Resume & Europass:
**Q: Do I need to log in to create a resume?**
A: Yes, login is required to create, edit, and download your resume for security and personalization.

**Q: Can I download my resume in multiple formats?**
A: Yes! You can download as Europass CV (PDF) or Standard Resume (PDF).

**Q: Can I edit my resume after creating it?**
A: Yes, you can edit anytime. Go to Resume ‚Üí Create/Edit Resume.

### Job Search:
**Q: Why don't I see any job results?**
A: Try using different keywords or a broader location. Job availability varies by location and field.

**Q: Do I need to log in to search for jobs?**
A: No, you can search without logging in, but you need to log in to apply for jobs.

**Q: How do I track my applications?**
A: Click "My Applications" in the menu (available only when logged in).

### Investment Programs:
**Q: How long does visa processing usually take?**
A: Processing times vary by country and program, from 2 weeks to 36+ months. Check the specific program details.

**Q: What is the minimum investment required?**
A: Varies by country and program. Some programs require investments starting from USD 50,000+.

**Q: Can my family come with me?**
A: Most investment programs allow family members. Check the "Benefits" section for each program.
