Nexora — Global Residency & Visa Platform

Nexora helps individuals and families discover, compare and apply for residency and visa programs worldwide. We aggregate up-to-date program data (investment levels, processing times, documentation, family eligibility, visa-free access and citizenship pathways) and provide practical tools to evaluate options:

- Smart Eligibility Checker: match your budget, family needs and goals.
- Program Comparison: compare multiple programs side-by-side.
- ROI & Cost Calculator: estimate total costs and projected returns.
- Consultant Network: connect with verified immigration advisors.
- Application Tracking: manage application steps and documents in one place.
